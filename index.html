( Profile )

Hello, I am Lilian Zhou.

I am a UX / UI / interaction experience designer with a background across art technology, information experience and digital products. Based in Shanghai, I work on social products, AI assistants and web experiences, with additional experience in automotive services and intelligent cockpit projects.

I like turning complex problems into experiences that feel natural.

I start from user scenarios, information architecture and interaction prototypes, then carry ideas through testing and development. I also use AI tools to validate design concepts quickly. For me, good design is not only visual: it should be clear, reliable and genuinely useful.

I care about responsive, emotionally aware design.

Whether it is a conversation, a button, sound, light or space, I want systems to give the right feedback at the right moment, making the relationship between people and products feel more natural and warmer.
